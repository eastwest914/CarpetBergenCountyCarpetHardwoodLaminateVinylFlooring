Is the FREE premium padding really included with every carpet?

Absolutely, yes. Every carpet we install comes with top-of-the-line premium bonded padding at no extra cost. This is our standard — not an upgrade, not a promotion. It is included with every single carpet installation we do. Premium padding makes your carpet feel better, perform better and last longer.

What flooring brands do you carry?

We carry the most respected brands in the flooring industry including Mohawk, Shaw, Beaulieu and Congoleum — all unsurpassed in quality and remarkable beauty. These brands cover every category from carpet and hardwood to laminate, vinyl and luxury vinyl tile.

Do you offer shop at home service?

Yes, we offer a convenient shop at home service. Our team will bring samples directly to your home so you can see how the flooring looks in your actual space, under your lighting and next to your furniture. This makes it much easier to make the right decision before installation begins.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer special financing programs to help make your flooring project more affordable. Ask us about financing when you visit our showroom or call us at 201-301-0688. We want to help you get the beautiful floors you deserve within your budget.

Do you do hardwood floor resurfacing?

Yes, we specialize in wood floor resurfacing. If your existing hardwood floors are looking worn, scratched or dull, resurfacing can restore them to like-new condition for a fraction of the cost of replacement. Our team will assess your floors and recommend the best approach to bring back their original beauty.
